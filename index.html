Career Profile

Currently I’m working at Cegeka as a PAM / IAM Architect, designing and building solutions for our customers. Before I started working at Cegeka, I was a PAM Solution Engineer for 2,5 years at Proximus where I worked on the IPRAM project. This project was started to get in control of Proximus’ high privileged accounts.

Previously I was an identity and access management consultant for 9 years where the main focus laid on Federation. I worked on several larger projects designing and building the solution as well as providing support for these solutions.

One of the bigger projects I worked on was building and supporting the FAS/CSAM service which enables Belgian citizens to interact online with the Belgian government. This by providing identification, authentication, authorisation and mandate management. Besides being involved in identity and access management projects, I also set up several cloud environments including the management of our own lab environment.

Furthermore, I’m also an avid sports enthusiast and proud blood donor. I like to take photos as much as possible and I volunteered at the Special Olympics European Summer Games.
